BAMS Doctors

Description

We are seeking dedicated BAMS (Bachelor of Ayurvedic Medicine and Surgery) doctors who are passionate Jobs providing holistic healthcare. BAMS doctors have completed their degree in Ayurveda and are trained to diagnose and treat various health conditions using traditional Ayurvedic practices. They play an essential role in promoting wellness and preventive care through natural therapies.

Key Responsibilities and Opportunities

As a BAMS doctor, your responsibilities will include:

  • Conducting patient assessments and diagnosing health issues.
  • Developing personalized treatment plans based on Ayurvedic principles.
  • Providing counseling on diet, lifestyle, and preventive care.
  • Administering Ayurvedic therapies and treatments.
  • Engaging in community health education initiatives.
